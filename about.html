Our Story

AI Solutions is headquartered in Sunderland, United Kingdom. We harness artificial intelligence to help organisations across industries deliver software solutions that rapidly and proactively address issues affecting the digital employee experience — accelerating design, engineering, and innovation along the way.

Our integrated AI-powered virtual assistant responds to user inquiries in real time, alongside affordable, AI-driven prototyping services. Together, these capabilities help clients move from idea to impact faster — with measurable improvements in speed, quality, and employee satisfaction.

Our Journey

Company History

From our Sunderland headquarters to partnerships across the UK and beyond — delivering trusted AI for the modern workplace.

2022
Founded
AI Solutions Established in Sunderland

AI Solutions was founded in Sunderland by a small team of AI engineers and UX specialists with one goal — to use artificial intelligence to improve how people experience technology at work.

2023
Product Launch
AI Virtual Assistant Released

Launched our flagship AI-powered virtual assistant, enabling organisations to respond to user inquiries instantly and reduce friction in everyday digital workflows.

2024
Innovation
Prototyping Platform & DEX Focus

Introduced affordable, AI-driven prototyping services and sharpened our focus on the digital employee experience — helping clients proactively resolve workplace technology issues before they escalate.

2025
Growth
