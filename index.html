About Us

Who We Are

A Sunderland-based technology company using AI to transform the digital employee experience for organisations worldwide.

Our Story

AI Solutions is headquartered in Sunderland, United Kingdom. We use AI to help industries deliver software solutions that rapidly and proactively address issues affecting the digital employee experience — speeding up design, engineering, and innovation.

Our integrated AI virtual assistant answers user inquiries on demand, while our affordable prototyping services help teams move from concept to delivery with confidence — giving organisations a practical edge in today's competitive digital landscape.

Our Mission

Our mission is to innovate, promote, and deliver the future of the digital employee experience — with a strong focus on supporting people at work. Through global expansion, we aim to make a worldwide impact and help every organisation build a smarter, more human-centred workplace.
